Prices of petrol and diesel remained steady:

Trivandrum:Prices of petrol and diesel remained steady for the 19th day in a row on April 25. Since the end of a four-and-a-half-month long hiatus in rate revision on March 22, rates of petrol and diesel have increased by Rs 10 per litre each via 14 revisions. Fuel prices were last hiked on April 6 by 80 paise a litre each.
